BombGuys

This is a remake of Bomberman.

I taught a basic version of this game in my Video Game Programming class. I personally took that basic version and added a lot more to it. I added the ability to choose from 4 characters, added a small variety of powerups and half-implemented up to four players to play a game. Currently, if you choose to play with more than 2 players, the players will load with their chosen avatars, but there are no controls mapped to players 3 and 4.

If/when I revisit this project, I'll add in the ability to play with game pads and flesh out the map(s).
